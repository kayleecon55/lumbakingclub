About Us

What is the Loyola Baking Club?

The baking club at Loyola University Maryland is a club where students can participate in seasonal baking events, meetings, and gatherings. No prior knowledge is needed; members range from beginner to advanced. Events include bake sales, fundraisers, tabling events, and more.
